Data Scientist — Consumer Insights Team
TechCorp Inc. | Chicago, IL | Full-Time

About the Role
We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our Consumer Insights team. You will build machine
learning models, design A/B experiments, and surface actionable insights that drive product
decisions for 10M+ users.

Responsibilities
- Design and deploy machine learning models end-to-end in a production environment
- Conduct A/B testing and causal inference studies to measure feature impact
- Build and maintain data pipelines using Python and Spark
- Communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders via dashboards and presentations
- Partner with product and engineering teams on data-driven roadmap decisions

Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
- 1+ years of hands-on experience with Python for data analysis and modeling
- Proficiency in SQL for querying large datasets
- Experience with machine learning frameworks (scikit-learn, XGBoost, or LightGBM)
- Strong understanding of statistical concepts: hypothesis testing, regression, and probability
- Experience with A/B testing and experimental design

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with Spark or distributed computing frameworks
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with MLflow or another experiment tracking tool
- Knowledge of causal inference methods (diff-in-diff, propensity score matching)
- Prior experience with LLM APIs or generative AI tools

Technical Skills
Python, SQL, scikit-learn, XGBoost, Spark, AWS, Tableau, Git, Docker, MLflow
